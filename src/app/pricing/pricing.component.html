Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a Connection?

A Connection represents each Salesforce org or environment that Data Sync Pro connects to. Most customers need connections for their sandboxes plus production.

What is an Executable?

An Executable is a modular rule for your data management process, such as ETL operations, Batch Jobs, Automations, Data Lists, Action Buttons, or Data Loaders.

Are there any user limits?

No! Data Sync Pro uses a site-wide license model, meaning unlimited users within your Salesforce org can access and use the platform.

How does billing work?

Billing is monthly based on your active Connections and Executables. You can scale up or down as your needs change.
