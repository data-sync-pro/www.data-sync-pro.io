Frequently Asked Questions

How do the subscription tiers work?

Choose a core tier based on your org structure and batch processing needs. Starter for single-org, Standard for multi-org, and Batch tiers for high-volume processing. Add usage-based add-ons as needed.

What counts as a Connection?

A Connection represents each Salesforce org or environment that Data Sync Pro connects to. The Standard tier includes 5 connections (4 sandboxes + 1 production), while additional connections cost $600/month each.

What is an Executable?

An Executable is a modular rule for your data management process, such as ETL operations, Batch Jobs, Automations, Data Lists, Action Buttons, or Data Loaders. All plans include 100 executables, with additional ones at $10/month each.

Are there any user limits?

No! All Data Sync Pro plans use a site-wide license model with unlimited users. Everyone in your Salesforce org can access and use the platform at no additional cost.

How does batch processing pricing work?

All plans include 20k records/day batch processing. Upgrade to Batch Premium (1M records/day) or Batch Unlimited for higher volumes, or add batch capacity à-la-carte for $2,000-$5,000/month.

Can I scale up or down?

Yes! You can change your subscription tier or add/remove usage-based add-ons as your needs evolve. Billing adjusts monthly based on your active configuration.
