Data Sync Pro is licensed on a site-wide basis, allowing unlimited users within your Salesforce org, maximizing value across teams without incurring per-user (per-seat) costs.

Pricing is based on three factors:

  • Connections – Each Salesforce org connected through DSP, including your current and any external orgs.
    $600 per connection per month

  • Executables – The rules or logic units created in DSP to define data operations.
    $10 per executable per month

  • Daily Data Volume – The total number of records processed each day:

    • 20k records/day → Included

    • 1M records/day → $2,000/month

    • Salesforce platform limit → $5,000/month

This model ensures pricing scales with the scope of your data management—based on how many orgs are connected, how much logic you configure, and how much data you process.
