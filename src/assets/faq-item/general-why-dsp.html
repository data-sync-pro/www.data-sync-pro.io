Unlike tools that simply swap code for drag-and-drop screens while still leaving complexity to the designers, DSP is rules-driven, modular, and record-based at its core. This approach simplifies business logic, promotes single responsibility principle, and enforces clear boundaries—delivering streamlined processes, simpler systems, and enterprise-scale performance across every Salesforce org.

Key Benefits

  • Great Performance – Batch jobs up to 20× faster
    DSP's Batch engine is optimized for Salesforce governor limits, executing jobs up to 20× faster than Apex batch. It supports complex transformations, cleansing, and large-scale operations at enterprise speed.

  • Bulkified Transformation – 140+ functions
    Leverage 140+ built-in functions & variables—covering strings, dates, math, booleans, lookups, regex, roll-ups, masking, triggers and extend the library thru an Apex interface for the most tailored transformations.

  • Streamlining & Empowering – Covers most customization needs
    With five rules engines—#Batch, #Trigger, #Data List, #Action Button, and #Data Loader—and actions such as #Insert, #Update, #Delete, #Undelete, #Merge, #Publish, #Lead Convert, #Notifications, and #Approvals, DSP addresses most customization scenarios while cutting build effort by up to 90%.

  • Rules-Driven Simplicity
    Business logic is expressed as clear, declarative rules—significantly reducing complexity for architects, admins, and developers.

  • Modular & Record-Based
    Rules are stored as structured records—bounded, reusable, and easy to maintain. Each module defines clear inputs and outputs for predictable scalability.

  • Enterprise Security – Local Processing
    All execution runs natively inside Salesforce, ensuring compliance and eliminating external data movement.

  • Drastically Reduced Technical Debt
    The modular, record-based framework replaces tightly-coupled Apex and Flow implementations with clean, bounded logic. By isolating complexity and enforcing single responsibility, DSP drastically lowers long-term technical debt.

With DSP, you gain a single, powerful platform to implement, maintain, and scale Salesforce data processes—faster, safer, and simpler with long-term scalablity.
